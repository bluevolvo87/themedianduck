The Median Duck Home Page
================

# Welcome!

Welcome to The Median Duck, a self initiated project that aims to
educate and entertain the general public of data analytic topics through
data from the hit UK TV show “Taskmaster”.

This project is in its early stages, but great things are planned; see
this [vision document](Vision_Document.md) for the ambitious plans I
have in mind
