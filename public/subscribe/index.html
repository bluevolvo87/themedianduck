A Josh Widdicombe Counting Baked Beans Approach

  1. Choose your preferred RSS feed e-mailer platform: feedrabbit.com or blogtrottr.
    • Signing up to these services may be required.
  2. In the “URL of a feed or web site” input field (or similarly labelled), provide the following address: https://bluevolvo87.github.io/themedianduck/index.xml.

Some considerations:

  • feedrabbit.com is my preferred platform in my opinion.
    • The e-mails are minimalistic, with no-ads embedded into e-mail.
    • However, the feed does appear to be monitored less and you may not get the latest post e-mail as quickly (a few hours delay).
  • blogtrottr does seem to be quicker in providing update e-mails.
    • However, the e-mails are less streamlined with ads embedded into the e-mail.
