Extra, Extra! Read All About It!

Readers!

Have you found it highly inconvenient to find out if there is a newly published Median Duck post? If yes, then fear no more!1

You can now be e-mailed as to when a new post has been published. Simply use the RSS feed information associated with this site, and use feedrabbit.com to be e-mailed as to when there is a new post. Other RSS feed e-mailers are available (follow.it, blogtrottr).

Signing up can be achieved in 2 simple steps! 2

  1. Choose your preferred RSS feed e-mailer platform: feedrabbit.com, follow.it or blogtrottr.
    • Signing up to these services may be required.
  2. In the “URL of a feed or web site” input field (or similarly labelled), provide the following address: https://bluevolvo87.github.io/themedianduck/index.xml.

Some considerations:

  • Additional steps to sign up and confirm usage of these RSS feed e-mailers may be required.
  • By going with a external third party RSS e-mailer, The Median Duck does not have access to your e-mail address or personal information that you provided.
    • This is great for readers who do not want to share their details with a mysterious blog poster who has questionable stats, writing and musical skills.
  • These dedicated third party e-mailers also allow for the reader to control the frequency in which they receive e-mails.
    • The reader can control how frequently they are spammed, and confounded by the contents featured on this blog.

Be the First to Say “First!”

Readers!

Have you ever wanted to:

  • interact with the writer of The Median Duck, but still remain safely anonymous?
  • interact with rest of The Median Duck community (henceforth known as “The Flock”)?
  • share your opinion on a Median Duck post?
  • point out incoherent (and correct) English that frequently occurs on this blog?
  • violently object to a statistical method or conclusion that is being stated?
  • add a redundant “First!” comment on a Median Duck post?

…then look no further! Commenting on posts has now been enabled for The Median Duck.

Thanks to cusdis, a “lightweight, privacy-first, open-source comment system”, all posts now have a comment section at the very bottom of the page.

Figure 3: How I anticipate the comment section to progress over time.

Some considerations:

  • No additional sign up is required to yet another third party website.
  • The Median Duck doesn’t store any private information on the commenter.
    • You (the reader) can stay anonymous behind the safety of your own screen!
    • Trolls and spammers can also rejoice at the low barrier of commenting entry.
  • Comments will be vetted and reviewed by the webmaster:
    • Readers can no doubt expect some biased comment selection here.
